Our Philosophy

Simplicity Is the Highest Form of Craft

Mochi Atelier was born from a deep reverence for Japanese wagashi culture — the art of expressing nature through confection. We believe the finest sweets require patience, precision, and the humility to let ingredients speak for themselves.

Every mochi is made by hand each morning. We source glutinous rice flour from a family mill in Niigata, and our fillings change with the seasons — cherry blossom in spring, roasted chestnut in autumn, yuzu in winter.
